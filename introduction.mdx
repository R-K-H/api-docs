## What is Futarchy AMM?

Futarchy AMM is an automated market maker implementation for MetaDAO that enables futarchic governance through prediction markets.

## Key Features

- **Constant Product AMM**: Uniswap V1-style pricing with 0.5% fees
- **Automatic Arbitrage**: Built-in arbitrage between spot and conditional markets
- **TWAP Oracle**: Manipulation-resistant price feeds
- **Conditional Markets**: Pass/fail markets for proposal outcomes
- **Fee Structure**: 0.25% to LPs + 0.25% to protocol
